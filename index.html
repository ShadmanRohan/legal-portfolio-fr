01 — ABOUT

Profile

I am a practicing Advocate at the Bangladesh Supreme Court — admitted to both the High Court Division and Appellate Division — with over 25 years of experience in fiscal law, taxation, corporate compliance, civil and criminal litigation, and financial crime advisory work.

As partner at Judicium Barristers & Advocates and a member of the Rule of Law Jurist Syndicate, I have built a practice grounded in the financial and regulatory architecture of Bangladesh. My work as legal and financial advisor to the Bangladesh Army Welfare Trust, Sena Kalyan Sangstha, and over 600 national and international organisations has given me a sustained view of how governance gaps create conditions for financial crime.

Since 2024, I have published six peer-reviewed articles examining the intersection of artificial intelligence, criminal justice, and financial crime governance — work that directly informs my ongoing doctoral research.

Doctoral Research · In Progress
